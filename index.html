FAQ

Questions traders ask first.

Do you hold my funds?

No. Strategies trade through your own exchange API keys with withdrawals disabled. Hyperliquid uses scoped signer authority, while GMX requires connected-wallet review and signature for each real-money transaction. Tradign never requests your primary-wallet seed phrase or private key.

Can it trade without my permission?

Only if you choose Direct mode. In Approval mode every entry waits for your confirmation on Telegram or email before anything executes, and Paper mode never touches real capital at all.

How do I know a strategy works before risking money?

Backtest it first and review trades, net P&L, win rate, max drawdown, historical coverage and modeled trading costs. Then paper trade it live. Going live is the last step, not the first.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. Describe the idea in plain English and the AI maps it onto a visual strategy graph. If you want manual control, 50+ drag-and-drop blocks cover indicators, price action, logic and risk.

Which exchanges are supported?

Binance, Bybit, OKX, Bitget and KuCoin futures. Hyperliquid uses scoped signer authority; GMX uses connected-wallet review and signature for each transaction. dYdX execution remains gated.
