Both sides of the permission line, spelled out.

✓What Tradign can do

Read market data and account balances for dashboards
Place and manage orders on venues you connected
Execute Hyperliquid trades through scoped signer authority; prepare GMX transactions for your wallet to review and sign
Pause, resume or stop strategies instantly, on your command

✗What it can never do

Withdraw or transfer funds. The permission does not exist
Request your primary-wallet private key, seed phrase or recovery phrase
Echo a stored secret back. Keys are encrypted server-side, never shown in chat
Keep access you revoked. Released keys stop strategies safely

How secrets live here

01

Encrypted server-side

API keys and provider secrets are encrypted at rest, never rendered in the UI again, and never shared in chat.

02

Paper before capital

Every strategy can run against live markets with zero permissions and zero capital before you connect anything real.

03

Approval before entry

In Approval mode every entry waits for your one-tap confirmation on Telegram or email. Skip it and nothing happens.

04

Revoke in one click

Releasing credentials blocks new entries. Existing exposure remains tracked and requires reconciliation until it is flat.
