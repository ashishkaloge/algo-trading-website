Four layers of failing safely

Design choices that matter more than a green dashboard.

01

Paper and live are isolated

Paper execution carries no exchange or Web3 permissions at all, so nothing in a test can ever touch capital.

02

Deployments recover themselves

If a deployment is interrupted mid-flight, recovery services restore its state instead of leaving a strategy half-live.

03

Execution changes pause first

When execution targets change, new real trades pause until you confirm the new setup. The system never guesses with your money.

04

Approvals fail closed

In Approval mode, if a confirmation never arrives, nothing executes. Silence means no trade. Always.
