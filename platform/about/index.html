How it got here

The itch

Strategies lived in spreadsheets and screenshots

Ideas were tested by hand, executed late, and shared as unverifiable screenshots. The tools were either black-box bots or code-first platforms.

The build

One workspace, end to end

Chat, a visual graph builder, backtesting with costs, paper trading, and live execution grew into a single flow, with approval gates designed in from the start, not bolted on.

The breadth

Five exchanges and Web3

One execution layer supports Binance, Bybit, OKX, Bitget and KuCoin futures. Hyperliquid uses scoped signer authority; GMX keeps each transaction wallet-reviewed and signed. dYdX execution remains gated.

Now

Live, and raising

The product is live at tradign.com with free building and paper trading. We are raising to grow the community and ship AI Agents.

Trading bots became black boxes. Copy trading became blind faith. We think the answer is a system you can read, where every strategy is a visible graph, every result carries verifiable proof, and every live deployment begins with your explicit choice of account, risk and execution mode.
